welcome to bey's world

in this adventure, you are beyonce's personal assistant. today is a big day, you are preparing beyonce to do a show in the Colosseum. any choice you make can get you fired, either immediately, or later in the story. try not to get fired, your reputation depends on it!

your first task of the day has to do with breakfast. beyonce wakes up and asks for food. do you feed her:

  1. something hearty; eggs, bacon, grits, sausage, spinach, texas toast, fresh orange juice, water
  2. something light; fruit parfait, cayenne lemon water, a protein bar
  3. something sweet; fresh baked cinnamon rolls, strawberries and blueberries, whipped cream, a cappucino, water
